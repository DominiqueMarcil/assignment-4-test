Description of concept and art directionThis project draws inspiration from the profound philosophies of Franz Kafka and Democritus,
an eminent figure in ancient Greek philosophy, particularly known for his contributions to atomistic theory. The design embodies
the essence of German design aesthetics, characterized by its emphasis on existentialism and deep philosophical inquiry. The square and
boxy layout pays homage to the precision and clarity often associated with German design principles. Through a surrealist approach to typography,
the words in this project are orchestrated to elicit a sense of confusion and introspection, mirroring the existential torment often encountered in German philosophy. Just as German philosophy challenges and provokes the mind, this design seeks to engage the
viewer in a thought-provoking and immersive experience.
